The Experience

From meditative sonic journeys to thunderous group battles, explore the many ways we connect through rhythm.

Community Drum Circles

Our signature event. Hundreds of people sitting in concentric circles, armed with djembes, congas, and shakers, guided by a facilitator into an ecstatic state of collective music-making.

Rhythm Experiences

Immersive sonic journeys combining traditional percussion with modern soundscapes. Close your eyes, let the beat guide you, and experience the meditative power of continuous rhythm.

Interactive Group Activities

Team-building exercises designed to break down barriers. Through call-and-response, syncopation games, and body percussion, groups learn to listen and collaborate in real-time without words.

Learning Basic Beats

Master the fundamentals. We break down complex global rhythms into accessible elements. Learn proper hand technique, timing, and how to hold a groove with absolute confidence.

Rhythm Face-Offs

Friendly, high-energy percussion battles where sections of the crowd trade complex rhythms. It's call-and-response turned up to eleven, ending in a massive, unified crescendo.
